Acression Network aims to create a decentralized digital asset financing and exchange network through Acression Protocol.

The Acression Protocol is divided into two layers: 1) Basic protocol layer, which mainly includes upper layer financial business registration, configuration, routing and management. 2) The asset-based, asset protocol layer completes user-defined financial business through the implementation of the terms contract interfaces.
